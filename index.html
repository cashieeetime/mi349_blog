Classic Brick Cottage

Posted by cashie, on October 16th, 2022

This is a simple brick starter house that is aesthetic and small enough for one person. The farm on the right is an old fashioned one, with a simple roof to protect your crops. The interior of the first floor is open, with a bookshelf nook in the corner. The bedroom is up in the attic, with glass windows on the ceiling.
